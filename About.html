About Us

Advance Academy Coding Club is a student-led organization dedicated to promoting computer science education and providing opportunities for students to explore and develop their coding skills. We host events, workshops, and hackathons throughout the year, and work on various projects to improve our skills and contribute to the community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire and empower students to become active participants in the digital world by providing opportunities to learn and create with technology. We strive to create an inclusive and supportive environment for all students to explore their interests and develop their skills in computer science.
