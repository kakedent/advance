Event 1: Annual Advance Academy Project Showcase

Every year Advance Academy hosts a cometition for students to showcase their progress, a Panel of judges will evaluate each team's(maximum of two members) demonstration and rank them from bronze to gold based on their quality, with respect to the student's own capabilites

Learn More

Rewards

The prizes for each tier are:

Tier 3(Bronze): Prize budget of 25$ for each member

Tier 2(Silver): Prize budget of 50$ for each member

Tier 1(Gold): Prize budget of 100$ for each member

The projects will be judged by a panel of 3 judges (Mr. Lee, Eric Bae, and Richard Durbin). There is no limit to winners of each Tier, so if every project is on a gold level, everyone will recive the maximum reward

When

You will have from December 16th to Janurary 13th to develop your projects, you are able to finish mostly unfinished projects(can not be almost complete)

Presentation

At the end of the 4 week period, each project will be presented by the creators and then evaluated by the judges. The expectation is that it relates to coding and utilizes the cumulation of all your skills and experience. At the halfway mark there will be a "review" period where peers will share their projects and recive new strategies and ideas for inspiration.
